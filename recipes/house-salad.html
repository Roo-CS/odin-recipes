House Salad

Description

 An Italian house salad is a refreshing and vibrant dish that brings together the simple, fresh flavors of Mediterranean cuisine. Crisp romaine lettuce serves as the base, providing a crunchy and refreshing foundation for the salad. Juicy cherry tomatoes add a burst of natural sweetness, while thinly sliced cucumbers bring a cool, crisp texture. Red onions, sliced paper-thin, lend a zesty bite that complements the other ingredients beautifully. Together, these elements create a colorful and inviting salad that is as visually appealing as it is flavorful.

 To elevate the dish, the salad is lightly tossed in a classic Italian dressing made from olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic, and a medley of herbs like oregano and basil. The dressing enhances the natural flavors of the fresh vegetables without overpowering them, striking the perfect balance of tangy, savory, and herby notes. Often garnished with a sprinkle of freshly grated Parmesan cheese or croutons for added texture, this Italian house salad is a versatile side dish that pairs wonderfully with a variety of main courses, from pasta to grilled meats.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce Mix
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red Onion
  • Italian Dressing

Steps

  1. Add the lettuce mix to the bowl
  2. Cut up cherry tomatoes
  3. Cut up cucumber
  4. Cut up red onion
  5. Add all cut up vegetables to the bowl
  6. Add italian dressing
  7. Mix well and serve
